Frequently Asked Questions

Is "Performance Coaching" different from traditional therapy?
While therapy focuses on healing past challenges, performance coaching is future-oriented — optimizing your current strengths for specific goals.
Can I switch between online and in-person sessions?
Absolutely. Flexibility is key — choose whatever best fits your energy and schedule.
How do the workshops help with Emotional Intelligence?
You practice self-awareness, empathy, and communication in a safe, guided environment.
